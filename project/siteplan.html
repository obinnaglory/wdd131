Glory's CleanTown Water Initiative

Community-focused water system planning, education, and volunteer resources.

Optional domain: cleantownwater.org

Site Purpose

This site will serve as the project hub for the CleanTown Water Initiative. It will provide:

  • Project overview and timeline for building the town's elevated water tank.
  • Technical resources and downloadable engineering plans for volunteers.
  • Volunteer sign-up and scheduling for installation and maintenance tasks.
  • Community FAQ and contact form for residents seeking water access information.

Scenarios (User Questions)

  1. Where can I volunteer next weekend?
    Users need an easy way to find upcoming volunteer shifts and sign up.
  2. How much water pressure will I get at my home?
    Residents want a calculator or simplified explanation of expected water pressure based on their location.
  3. Where can I download the engineering drawings?
    Contractors and technical volunteers need a downloads page with versioned CAD/PDF files.

CSS & Files

  • Files: index.html (home), siteplan.html (this doc), css/styles.css, img/logo.svg, docs/engineering/.
  • Structure: A light utility-first layout with container, grid, and card components. Keep variables in root for color/spacing/typography.
  • Performance: Self-host fonts if possible (or preload Google Fonts). Minify CSS for production.

Testing & Validation Checklist

  • HTML validation: run pages through W3C Validator.
  • Accessibility: use axe or Lighthouse (contrast, semantic headings, ARIA where needed, form labels).
  • Color contrast: verify against WCAG 2.1 AA for text and UI elements.
  • SEO basics: title, meta description, structured data for events/volunteer opportunities.
  • Performance: Lighthouse scores, compress images (SVG for logos), lazy-load large assets.
  • Responsive checks: test on small phone, medium tablet, and large desktop breakpoints.
